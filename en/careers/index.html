Psychologist / Therapist

Individual sessions for adults, children, or couples ‚Äì stationary, online, or hybrid. Possibility to conduct workshops and crisis interventions.

  • Office equipped with project materials and psychological tests.
  • Individual and team supervision once a month.
  • Funding for certification and conferences.

Pedagogue / Special Educator

Educational, upbringing, and family support. Work in an interdisciplinary team and implementation of prevention programs in schools.

  • Flexible schedules tailored to school activities.
  • Access to psychoeducational materials and TPP programs.
  • Possibility of working with families in short-term processes.
Apply

Sensory Integration Therapist

Classes in a well-equipped SI room with the possibility of consultation with a psychologist and physiotherapist.

  • Assistant support in conducting classes.
  • Materials for working with parents and monitoring progress.
  • Possibility to expand competencies with hand therapy or TUS.
Apply

Psychiatrist / Anesthesiologist

Specialist consultations in psychosomatic and psycho-oncological projects. Hybrid work or guest visits.

  • Full administrative support and coordinator support.
  • Possibility to participate in research and publications.
  • Flexible settlement of visits (lump sum or hourly rate).

Program Coordinator / Project Leader

Conducting social campaigns, workshops, and publications. We are looking for people who want to develop in project management.

  • Mentoring in management and communication.
  • Project budgets and fundraising support.
  • Possibility of remote work (online coordination).

Intern / Volunteer

A program combining mentoring, training, and real tasks. Choice of path: media, research, events, educational projects.

  • Implementation program and meeting schedule.
