Psychologist / Therapist

Work with adults, adolescents or families on-site and online. Design workshops, crisis interventions or psychoeducation initiatives.

  • Therapy rooms equipped with diagnostic tools and materials.
  • Monthly individual and group supervision.
  • Funding for certifications, conferences and publications.

Pedagogue / Special educator

Support families, schools and youth groups. Co-create prevention programmes and work alongside psychologists in our hubs.

  • Flexible schedules aligned with school hours.
  • Access to psychoeducational resources and family support tools.
  • Opportunities to deliver group programmes and parenting workshops.

Sensory Integration Therapist

Conduct SI therapy in a fully equipped studio with support from psychology and physiotherapy teams.

  • Assistant support during sessions.
  • Ready-to-use progress tracking templates and home programmes.
  • Expansion options: hand therapy, social skills groups or parent coaching.

Psychiatrist / Anaesthesiologist

  • Full administrative support and dedicated coordinator.

  • Flexible billing (flat rate or hourly).

Frequently asked questions

Need more details? Drop us a note at rekrutacja@psychopedagog.org.

Which contract formats are possible? add

Most collaborations run on a B2B basis or civil contracts. Interns and volunteers sign a dedicated agreement aligned with Polish regulations.

Can I combine this role with another job? add

Yes. Many collaborators run a private practice or academic schedule alongside. We tailor slots to your availability.

How long does the recruitment process take? add

Typically 7–14 days. We reply within five business days after receiving your application and then schedule an interview.
