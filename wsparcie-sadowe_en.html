Judicial and Legal Support

Professional psychological services for court proceedings. We ensure reliability, discretion, and the highest standards.

Important Information Regarding English Services

Please note that our services in English are currently offered on a limited basis. To inquire about availability and schedule an online appointment with a suitable specialist, please send us an email at office@psychopedagog.org. We will respond to you as soon as possible.

Professional psychological assessments tailored to the needs of court proceedings. Our specialists conduct documentation analysis, interviews, and use diagnostic tools to provide reliable conclusions. We advise and support at every stage of the process, helping to prepare for hearings and understand the psychological aspects of the case.

We prepare specialized psychological opinions to support judicial and pre-judicial processes. Our team of experts ensures reliability and the highest ethical standards. Opinions can concern, among other things, parental competencies, violence risk assessment, or rehabilitation capacity, providing a valuable source of information for the court.

A specialized program for parents undergoing court proceedings. The course develops parenting and communication skills and helps cope with emotions in difficult family situations. Completing the course and receiving a certificate is important proof of commitment to the child's well-being.

A program supporting the development of interpersonal skills, stress management, and constructive conflict resolution. Aimed at individuals for whom improving social functioning is important in the context of court proceedings.

An educational-therapeutic program helping people struggling with addictions, especially participants in court proceedings. The course covers strategies for coping with cravings, identifying triggers, and rebuilding relationships.

A program for couples in court proceedings who are uncertain about the future of their relationship. The course helps in making an informed decision, improving communication, and building a strong foundation for the relationship, or preparing for an amicable separation.
