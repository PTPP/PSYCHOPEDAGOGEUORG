Our recruitment stages

  1. Online application — fill a short recruitment form and accept privacy policy.
  2. Initial selection — HR reviews applications; clear CV and motivation help greatly.
  3. Recruitment questionnaire — selected candidates fill an online questionnaire.
  4. Invitation to interview — interview usually online; we value openness.
  5. Interview — meet and talk; honesty matters.
  6. Decision or next step — we may invite for another stage or send the final decision.
  7. Welcome on board — onboarding and introduction to the team.
