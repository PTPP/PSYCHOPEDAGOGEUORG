Parental and Marital Support

We build bridges, not walls. Discover how to strengthen bonds and improve communication in your family and relationship.

Important Information Regarding English Services

Please note that our services in English are currently offered on a limited basis. To inquire about availability and schedule an online appointment with a suitable specialist, please send us an email at office@psychopedagog.org. We will respond to you as soon as possible.

Sessions supporting families in building healthy relationships, resolving conflicts, and improving communication. Therapy allows for understanding the emotions of each family member and jointly seeking solutions in a safe, supportive atmosphere.

The goal of family therapy is to help all family members better understand each other and develop healthier ways of coping with emotional difficulties and conflicts that can affect family relationships.

Support for couples who want to cope with conflicts, rebuild trust, and strengthen their relationship. Therapy helps in developing key communication skills and joint problem-solving, leading to deeper understanding and intimacy.

Individual sessions supporting parents in developing parenting skills and building positive, strong relationships with their children. We offer practical tools and strategies to help you become a more conscious and confident parent.
