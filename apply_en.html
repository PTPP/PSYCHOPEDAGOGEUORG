Who we are

We deliver comprehensive psychological, pedagogical and therapeutic support in Bolesławiec and online. We implement community programmes, publish educational resources and build prevention projects.

Who we are looking for

Psychologists, therapists, pedagogues, sensory integration specialists, psychiatrists, as well as students, interns and volunteers eager to develop their talent in social impact work.

How we collaborate

We focus on transparent cooperation, peer support and constant knowledge exchange. Every new team member receives an onboarding roadmap and access to modern digital tools.

Clinical specialists

  • Flexible cooperation model: B2B, contract or agreement.
  • Regular individual and group supervision.
  • Modern offices plus encrypted telehealth platform.
  • Funding for certifications, conferences and publications.

Our recruitment process

We keep every stage transparent and finish with actionable feedback. Typical recruitment takes 10–14 days.

  1. 1. Application & document review

    Submit the online form or email your CV to rekrutacja@psychopedagog.org. We respond within five business days.

  2. 2. Online conversation & task

    Meet the recruitment coordinator, learn more about the role and complete a short task relevant to your position.

  3. 3. Team meeting & offer

    Discuss cooperation details, schedule and development plans. After the final decision you receive a full onboarding pack.

Frequently asked questions

Need more information? Email us at rekrutacja@psychopedagog.org or call (+48) 733 379 538.

How do you onboard new people? add

The first two weeks focus on onboarding with the coordinator, shadowing experienced specialists and understanding procedures. After one month we review progress and update the development plan.

Are tools and documentation provided? add

Yes. We provide a secure case management system, templates, psychoeducational materials and licenses for remote work tools together with training.

Can I pitch my own social project? add

Definitely. Each quarter we host a project pitch session. We help you design the budget, timeline and outreach, and we fund outstanding initiatives.

Ready to take the next step?

Complete the application form or reach out directly. We will gladly talk about your goals and find the right role for you.
