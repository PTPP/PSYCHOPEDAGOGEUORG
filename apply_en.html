Become a Specialist

Are you an experienced psychologist, pedagogue, or therapist looking for a place where your work has real meaning? Join our team of experts! We offer not just a job, but a path of dynamic development in an established organization.

  • Stable employment and a solid salary.
  • Full supervisory support and access to training.
  • Free certification as a Psychological Counselor.
  • Opportunity to implement your own projects.

Internships

Are you taking your first steps in the world of psychology and pedagogy? Do you want to gain valuable experience under the guidance of the best practitioners? Our internship programs are the perfect start to your career.

  • Flexible internships, both on-site and online.
  • Mentoring and support from a dedicated supervisor.
  • Real tasks and participation in interesting projects.
  • Transparent recruitment process based on motivation and knowledge.
  • Funding for courses and paid certification for the most committed.
