1. Controller and contact details

The controller of your personal data is the Psychopedagogical Society (ul. Warszawska 1/3, 59-700 Bolesławiec, Poland). For any questions related to data protection please email rekrutacja@psychopedagog.org, call (+48) 733 379 538 or send a letter to the address above with “Personal data – recruitment” in the subject.

This policy applies to all recruitment processes for employment, project-based cooperation, internships and volunteering carried out by the Psychopedagogical Society.

2. Purposes and legal bases

We process candidate data in order to:

  • check_circleconduct recruitment for the role you applied for (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – steps prior to entering into a contract),
  • check_circleassess qualifications, organise interviews and maintain communication (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – our legitimate interest),
  • check_circlecreate a talent pool for future opportunities when you give consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR),
  • check_circlecomply with legal obligations under labour and volunteering regulations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).

Consents

If you provide information beyond what is required by law (for example a photo or sensitive data), please attach consent for processing such details. You can withdraw any consent at any time; it will not affect processing carried out before withdrawal.

3. Categories of personal data

Depending on the role and application form, we may process:

Identification and contact data

Name, email, phone number, correspondence address, preferred form of cooperation and information about your availability.

Recruitment data

CV, cover letter, education, work experience, certificates, references, portfolios and responses given in application forms.

Technical data

IP address, timestamps, document metadata, interview notes and communication history.

Special categories

We do not require sensitive data. If you decide to share it (e.g. to request workplace adjustments), it will be processed solely on the basis of your consent and only for a justified purpose.

4. Recipients and international transfers

We may share data with trusted providers supporting our recruitment:

  • arrow_rightIT and hosting providers (e.g. Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, applicant tracking tools),
  • arrow_rightaccounting or legal firms when necessary,
  • arrow_rightpartner organisations if the opportunity is run jointly – such information appears in the job description.

When data leaves the European Economic Area, we rely on adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses and implement additional safeguards where required.

5. Retention periods

  • scheduleCurrent recruitment – until completion and up to 12 months afterwards to handle claims.
  • scheduleTalent pool – up to 24 months or until consent is withdrawn.
  • scheduleContract documentation – for the period required by tax and accounting regulations.

6. Your rights

You can request to:

Access and copy

Ask what data we process and obtain a copy.

Rectify or complete

Update inaccurate or incomplete information.

Erase (“right to be forgotten”)

Request deletion when data is no longer needed or consent has been withdrawn, unless another legal basis applies.

Restrict or object

Limit processing or object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Data portability & consent withdrawal

Receive data in a structured format, have it transmitted to another controller or withdraw consent whenever you wish. You can also lodge a complaint with the Polish Data Protection Authority.

7. Security and automated decisions

We use encrypted connections, access control, regular backups and incident response procedures. Data is stored on EU-based servers or on infrastructure protected with appropriate safeguards.

We do not make automated decisions about candidates based on profiling. Every application is reviewed by a human, and any practical tasks are only used to support our decision-making.

Need assistance with your data?

Email rekrutacja@psychopedagog.org or contact us via phone or form. We reply within 14 days.
