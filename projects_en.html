Our Mission in Action

Over the years, we have completed dozens of projects that have touched the lives of thousands. This is our story of commitment, passion, and real change. Discover how we are building a better tomorrow, project by project.

Crisis Interventions & Social Campaigns

The Mystery of Bartek's Death

We took action to clarify the tragic circumstances of Bartosz S.'s death in Lubin. Our evidence-gathering and media collaboration shed new light on the case, seeking justice.

No Prison for Krzysiek!

We organized a large-scale social campaign and fundraiser that led to the release and acquittal of a young man after a tragic accident. We showed the power of community support.

The Child's Choice!

Specialized, comprehensive care for a large family in an extremely difficult life situation, providing psychological support for children and parents.

A series of workshops building empathy and tolerance towards people with disabilities. Two editions of the project covered many institutions in Lower Silesia.

COVID-19 Support Hotline

In response to the pandemic crisis, we launched a free support and crisis intervention line for people affected by COVID-19 and isolation.
