Psychological and Dietetic Support

A holistic approach to your health, combining care for mind and body.

Important Information Regarding English Services

Please note that our services in English are currently offered on a limited basis. To inquire about availability and schedule an online appointment with a suitable specialist, please send us an email at office@psychopedagog.org. We will respond to you as soon as possible.

Professional psychological support for individuals struggling with emotional difficulties, interpersonal conflicts, and life challenges. Our specialists offer an individual approach and effective therapeutic strategies, helping to find inner balance and improve quality of life.

Remote psychological services providing access to specialists at a time and place convenient for you. We offer flexible solutions tailored to individual needs, ensuring comfort, discretion, and professionalism, no matter where you are.

Specialized psychological help for individuals struggling with addictions. We help understand the mechanisms of addiction, identify triggers, and build effective relapse prevention strategies. Our goal is to support you on your path to freedom from addiction.

Innovative consultations with a dietitian and psychodietitian that combine knowledge of nutrition and psychology. We support the creation of healthy eating habits, managing emotional eating, and achieving long-term health goals.
